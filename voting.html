WHAT I DID

I devloped Online Voting System as a part of my second year university mini project. The project was developed using HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, MySQL and PHPMyAdmin. The project was developed to provide a secure and easy to use platform for conducting the student council elections online.

My role was to develop the voting logic that let users securely login using their college Google Account and also vote for their favourite candidate. The project was developed in a team of 4 members and was completed in 2 months.

I also developed the admin panel where the admins can only monitor the total count for the number of votes and not the individual votes. The admin can also download the voting data in excel format.
