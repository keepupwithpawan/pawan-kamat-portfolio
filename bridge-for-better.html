WHAT I DID

Bridge for Better is a project developed as the submission for the Smart India Hackathon 2024. It was developed for the problem statement: Software solutions to reduce drop-out rate among students. We developed a platform that enables students to mention the reason for thier decision to drop out and what help they need to avoid this possibility.

Donors can be NGOs, Government Organiations or Private Donors who are willing to help these young individuals.
I was tasked with designing the UI of the website as well as develop the functionality of student registration and donor registration.
Donors can access the database of registered students and they can choose to help a student of their liking.

WHAT I LEARNED

Our research found out that most of the students who are dropping out are usually from villages and 3-tier cities where the most common reason of drop-out is parents not earning enough to support the household.

This forces the child to join thier parents in the work and hence they are unable to continue thier education. We also found out that most of the students are willing to continue thier education if they are given the opportunity.

This led to the idea that we will allow children to mention their problems and individuals with the power to help them are free to choose whoever they want to help according to what they can bring to the table.

We made this website very simple in terms of design and language as this will mostly be accessed by students and parents from small towns and villages who may not be tech-savy.
